About us

We are a team of professional and non-professional hikers, athletes and explorers, who all agreed on one thing - good outdoorsy jackets are too hard to come by. Therefore, we decided to take matter into our own hands, and now, three years later, we can proudly present our newly established brand - Rainydays.

Our brand focuses on comfort, affordability, inclusivity and durability, and we aim to make even more people seek the great outdoors, all while being comfy, warm and dry.
